Wenhan Yang (杨文瀚)


Wenhan Yang will be a postdoctoral researcher at Learning and Vision Lab, National University of Singapore. He received his PhD (July, 2018) in Computer Application Technology from Peking University. From September 2012 – July 2018, he was a Ph.D. candidate in Institute of Computer Science & Technology of Peking University (ICST). He is a member of Spatial and Temporal Restoration Understanding and Compression Team (STRUCT) at ICST.
